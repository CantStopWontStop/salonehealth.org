Salone Health

Salone Health comprises a diverse group of predominantly Sierra Leonean healthcare, public health, wellness, business, and design professionals.

Our primary objective is to educate, connect, and empower Sierra Leoneans and harness the power of the community around the world and take our physical and mental health into our own hands.

Missions

Salone Against COVID


With the number of COVID-19 cases rising around world, this initiative was launched to engage the Sierra Leonean diaspora, particularly the hard-hit communities in the US, and share information regarding the disease and staying healthy during the pandemic.
